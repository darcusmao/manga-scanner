Fonts used in this project
==========================

Bangers-Regular.ttf
  License:  SIL Open Font License 1.1 (OFL) — free for personal and commercial use
  Source:   https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Bangers
  GitHub:   https://github.com/google/fonts/tree/main/ofl/bangers
  Style:    Bold comic lettering, all-caps, excellent readability at small sizes

For production manga localization, consider replacing with:

  Anime Ace 2.0 (Blambot)
    License: Free for personal use only — NOT for commercial redistribution
    Source:  https://blambot.com/pages/free-fonts
    Style:   Industry standard manga/anime lettering

  CC Wild Words (Comicraft)
    License: Free for non-commercial use
    Source:  https://www.comicbookfonts.com/

Place the chosen .ttf file in this directory and update config.yaml:
  typesetting:
    font_path: "fonts/YourFont.ttf"
